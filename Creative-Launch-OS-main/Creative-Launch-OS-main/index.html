Common Questions

Do I need technical or business expertise to use Creative Launch OS?

+
No. Creative Launch OS is specifically designed for creative professionals without operational or technical backgrounds. The platform's AI-driven onboarding including automated portfolio analysis, style mapping, and SOP extraction requires no manual configuration. Simply upload your creative work and the system builds your business infrastructure automatically, handling HMRC compliance, lead triage, and client qualification on your behalf.

How is Creative Launch OS different from HoneyBook, Behance, or Contra?

+
Existing tools serve different functions: HoneyBook is a generic CRM requiring manual setup; Behance is a passive portfolio display platform; Contra is a marketplace for gig work. Creative Launch OS is the first platform that combines all three as a unified "Business Operating System" using your actual portfolio to train a style-bound AI that automates triage, qualification, and compliance. We are the infrastructure layer behind all other tools, not a replacement for any single one.

What does HMRC MTD compliance mean for my creative business?

+
From 2026, HMRC requires all UK sole traders and small businesses including creative freelancers to maintain digital records and file tax returns digitally through Making Tax Digital (MTD) compatible software. Creative Launch OS is built with a fully integrated HMRC MTD digital bridge, ensuring all your invoicing, record-keeping, and VAT/Income Tax reporting is automatically compliant no accountant required for routine digital filing.

How does Style Graphing protect my creative value against AI competition?

+
As AI tools flood the market with generic high-volume content, human creativity is being devalued unless it is uniquely authenticated. Creative Launch OS's Style Graphing engine analyses your specific aesthetic markers colour palettes, compositional style, tonal voice, technical signatures and maps them to commercial evidence that quantifies your "Creative Uniqueness." This gives you the data to defend premium pricing, prove originality to corporate clients, and build a defensible competitive moat that AI-generated content cannot replicate.
